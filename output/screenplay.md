# SCREENPLAY: UNTITLED
**Concept**: A lone astronaut unlocking a glowing ancient door on Mars.

## ACT II: Plot Point II - Discovery

**SCENE 1**
**SLUGLINE**: EXT. MARS RUINS - DAY
**CHARACTER**: Astronaut Rachel Kim

**ACTION**
Astronaut Rachel Kim approaches the ancient door, her footsteps echoing in the Martian landscape. The door, covered in intricate carvings, begins to glow with a soft, blue light.

**RACHEL**
What is this place? This can't be natural.

*(Emotional Shift: Rachel's initial trepidation gives way to fascination as she reaches out to touch the glowing door.)*

---

**ACTION**
As Rachel makes contact with the door, it slides open with a low hum, revealing a dark chamber beyond.

**RACHEL**
I'm reading some kind of energy signature coming from inside. It's not like anything I've seen before.

*(Emotional Shift: Rachel's curiosity deepens, but a hint of fear lingers, as she hesitates for a moment before stepping into the unknown.)*

---

**ACTION**
Rachel steps into the chamber, her suit's lights illuminating the space. The air is thick with an otherworldly energy, and she can feel it coursing through her veins.

**RACHEL**
This is incredible. I've never felt anything like it.

*(Emotional Shift: Rachel's sense of wonder and awe takes over, as she begins to explore the chamber and uncover its secrets.)*
